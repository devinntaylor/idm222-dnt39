Why do I do what I do

Since I was young, I have been fascinated by the connection humans are able to create through technology and design. Working with these two mediums allows designers and developers to collaborate and create what becomes an amazing experience for their user.

Through Drexel's Interactive Digital Media Program, I am gaining the knowledge which will allow me to apply my skills to any part of the development lifecycle. Whether it be research, design, developing, or testing, the opportunity this field has presented me with is unsurpassable.
