Septa System

A group project which aimed to redesign an automated system and enhance its user experience. Research, interaction, and implementation were key aspects of this assignment.

My group and I focused on redesigning the interfaces of the Septa kiosk, scanner, and phone app, along with the experience users have while interacting with it. Through research, we discovered that users were making errors due to inefficient signifiers that did not delineate clear functions or calls to action. Our solution involved addressing confusing language, clarifying icons and symbols, and reducing the number of clicks while maintaining a simple design.
